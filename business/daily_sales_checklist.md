# Daily Sales Checklist

## Morning

1. Open the live feed: https://shmghub.github.io/cybertender-radar/
2. Pick one high-confidence opportunity as today's hook.
3. Add 10 target companies to `business/outreach_tracker.csv`.
4. Send 10 personalized messages.

## Afternoon

1. Check Gumroad for new subscribers.
2. Send `business/customer_welcome_email.md` to any buyer.
3. Mark outreach rows as `sent`, `replied`, `not_fit`, or `subscribed`.

## Weekly Target

- 50 new targeted messages.
- 5 replies.
- 2 trials or direct sales conversations.
- 1 paid subscriber.
