Privacy

CyberTender Radar is a static discovery site. The public site does not run its own analytics, advertising pixels, account system, or tracking cookies.

Payments are handled by Gumroad. If you subscribe, Gumroad processes the checkout and customer account information under its own policies. Do not send sensitive procurement, password, bank, or private bid information through this site.

The opportunity data shown here comes from public official procurement and grant sources. The service links back to official notices so users can verify details before acting.
