Terms and Disclaimer

CyberTender Radar is a discovery and filtering tool for public-sector tender and grant opportunities. It is not a procurement authority, legal adviser, bid consultant, or guarantee of contract award.

Opportunity summaries, scores, values, deadlines, and confidence labels are generated from public data and may be incomplete, delayed, or wrong. Users must review the official source notice and tender documents before deciding whether to bid or apply.

Subscription access provides convenience, filtering, and exports. It does not guarantee that any subscriber will qualify for, apply for, or win any tender, grant, framework, or contract.

If an official source conflicts with CyberTender Radar, the official source controls.
