Company summary

CyberTender Radar turns public tender noise into cyber sales signals.

A premium procurement intelligence service for cybersecurity, MSP, IT services, cloud security, compliance, and digital forensics suppliers that want public-sector work without checking portals every morning.

What It Is

CyberTender Radar is a daily ranked feed of public cyber, IT, digital forensics, and security-related tender opportunities.

Who It Helps

Small suppliers that want public-sector contracts but do not have a dedicated bid-research team watching procurement portals.

Why It Exists

Good opportunities are scattered across official sources. The service makes them easier to scan, qualify, and act on.

How It Works

The feed collects official notices, scores them for cyber relevance, urgency, value, and SME fit, then publishes a live dashboard and CSV.

Brand Promise

Radar, score, act: find the right notices, rank them clearly, and turn them into a usable bid pipeline.

Identity

The Radar Shield mark is the final logo: a signal sweep inside a protective shield, built for trust and cyber intelligence.
